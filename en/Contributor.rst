Phraseanet is open source. You can contribute to the source code, translations, 
documentation, librairies or core.

Contributions to Phraseanet are opens, you can do your 
`pull-request <http://help.github.com/send-pull-requests/>`_ in the 
corresponding repositories.

- **Phraseanet**

  https://github.com/alchemy-fr/Phraseanet

  Please be sure to respect :doc:`the guidelines <Contributor/CodeGuidelines>`

- **Translation**

  https://translations.launchpad.net/phraseanettranslation/trunk/+pots/phraseanettrunktemplate

- **Documentation**
  
  https://github.com/alchemy-fr/Phraseanet-Docs

    - Sphinx Tutorial : http://matplotlib.sourceforge.net/sampledoc/
    - Markups Sphinx : http://sphinx.pocoo.org/markup/

* **API Client libraries**

  https://github.com/alchemy-fr/Phraseanet-Clients/
